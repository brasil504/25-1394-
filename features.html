Frequently Asked Questions

How do I care for my B-Lux sneakers?+

Wipe leather uppers with a damp cloth. Use a soft brush for mesh panels. Avoid machine washing. Store in the box when not in use to maintain shape.

Can I order custom colourways?+

Yes! Custom orders are available for individuals and bulk teams. Contact us via the Contact page with your preferred colourway and size requirements.

How long does delivery take outside Nairobi?+

East Africa: 3–5 days. Pan-Africa: 7–10 days. International: 14–21 days. Express courier options are available at checkout for faster delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept M-Pesa, Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, and select crypto currencies. Instalment plans are available on bulk orders above $300.

What is your return policy?+

Free returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn in original packaging. Defective items are replaced immediately under our 90-day guarantee.
