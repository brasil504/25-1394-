About B-LUX

Brasil Wahanda

My name is Brasil Wahanda, heir to the B-Lux One legacy. We specialize in creating stylish, comfortable, and affordable sneakers for everyone who demands more from their footwear.

Shoes are more than just something you wear — they are how you move through the world. They protect your feet, support your stride, and shape your comfort every single day.

At B-Lux, every stitch is intentional. Every sole is engineered for the streets. We don't follow trends — we create culture. Our designs emerge from the intersection of African streetwear, global sport, and luxury craftsmanship.

Our Philosophy

B-Lux is built on the belief that quality should never be a luxury reserved for the few. We are committed to making premium footwear accessible to every street, every city, every culture.

"From casual walks to big moments, the right pair makes all the difference. Style, function, and confidence start from the ground up."

Our design team draws inspiration from the vibrant streets of Nairobi, Lagos, Accra, and Johannesburg — cities alive with creativity and movement. We believe African street culture deserves world-class representation in the global sneaker space.

We are actively seeking regional distributors, brand ambassadors, and creative collaborators. Whether you are an athlete, an artist, or an entrepreneur — B-Lux has a place for you in our story.

A Word from the Founder

"B-Lux isn't just a shoe brand. It is a movement built from the ground up — one pair, one step, one street at a time. We are not chasing the world; the world is stepping into us."— Brasil Wahanda, Founder & Heir, B-Lux One Limited
